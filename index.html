How I got here

I started in Library and Information Science in Korea and now study at the UW iSchool. Along the way, I found that data is one of the best ways to improve how people learn and find what they need.

Relevant coursework: Cloud computing, data science, database systems, research methods.

What I'm building toward

I want to build tools that combine education, data, and AI in ways that feel simple and helpful. The goal is practical impact: better learning experiences and stronger communities.
